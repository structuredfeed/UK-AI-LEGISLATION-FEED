## UK Official Sources

This project uses only official UK government sources.

The National Archives (UK)
- Content Type: Acts of Parliament, Statutory Instruments, retained EU law
- Access Method: Official XML, RDF, CSV, and HTML endpoints
- Base URL: https://www.legislation.gov.uk
- License: Open Government Licence v3.0
- Notes: This is the authoritative source of UK law. All documents are retrieved in structured, machine-= readable formats provided by the publisher.

Department for Science, Innovation and Technology (DSIT)
- Publisher: UK Government
- Content Type: Policy papers, guidance, consultations, digital and data strategy documents
- Access Method: GOV.UK Content API
- Base URL: https://www.gov.uk/api/content
- License: Open Government Licence v3.0
- Notes: Data is retrieved exactly as published via GOV.UK’s official content delivery system.

Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)
- Publisher: Information Commissioner
- Content Type: Regulatory updates, enforcement summaries, guidance announcements
- Access Method: Official RSS feeds
- Base URL: https://ico.org.uk
- License: Open Government Licence v3.0
- Notes: Only officially published summaries and metadata are used. Full enforcement decisions are not scraped or reproduced.

Competition and Markets Authority (CMA)
- Publisher: UK Government
- Content Type: Competition policy updates, digital markets commentary, enforcement news
- Access Method: Official GOV.UK blog RSS feeds
- Base URL: https://competitionandmarkets.blog.gov.uk
- License: Open Government Licence v3.0
=Notes: Content is limited to publicly released blog updates and announcements.


Only metadata and summaries are processed. Full legal texts are not redistributed.
